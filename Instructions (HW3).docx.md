# HW3. Research Project

Each team chooses a research topic in dialogue systems and makes improvements. The improvement may be made to methods, data, applications, problem definitions, etc.

This homework consists of two parts: **proposal** and **video**.  
You do not need to submit a final report.

Each team should submit a proposal. The proposal will not contribute to your final grade.

### Content

The proposal may include (parts of) the following content. It should not exceed one page.

* Problem definition and motivation   
* Main idea/contributions/hypothesis  
* Literature survey  
* Proposed method  
* Proposed data  
* Proposed evaluation methods and metrics  
* Proposed timeline

### Rubric 

* Length 5%: Video should not exceed 15 minutes in length.  
* Clarity 10%: Presentation is delivered clearly.  
* Problem Definition 10%: Problem definition is clear and well motivated.  
* Related Work 10%: Closely related work is discussed and the current work is contextualized in the related work.  
* Method Novelty 10%: Proposed method (and data) is novel.  
* Method Soundness 15%: Proposed method and data are well motivated and sound.  
* Evaluation Metrics 10%: Evaluation uses proper metrics.  
* Evaluation Baselines 10%: Evaluation compares appropriate baselines.  
* Evaluation Analysis 20%: Analysis highlights the strengths of the work, and its limitations along with error analysis, and logical guidance for a future direction.
